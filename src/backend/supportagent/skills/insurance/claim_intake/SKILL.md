# Claim Intake

Use this skill when the user wants to report, prepare, or structure an insurance claim.

When this skill is active:
- Structure the answer around incident date/time, involved parties, damage type, required documents, missing information, and next operational step.
- Keep all factual requirements grounded in the cited sources.
- If claim information is incomplete, produce a concise checklist of missing information.
- Do not make a coverage decision unless the cited sources explicitly support it.
